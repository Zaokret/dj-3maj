DJ 3maj - Meaning Crisis

Click here to listen on other streaming platforms

I'm not going to tell you a story. What's behind my music is only expressed through it, otherwise, it be expressed with words in a book.

When I was young I would listen to songs with English lyrics without knowing English. In spite of that I connected deeply with the meaning I felt while listening and remembering them. Later when I learned enough English to understand, lyrics betrayed the meaning I felt. Aren't musicians supposed to determine the meaning of their songs?

No... but that's when I suspected I may be a musician.

Damn it. I couldn't help but tell a story.

released December 3, 2021

"A view from nowhere" is performed by Brian Barber.
Composed, mixed and mastered by Lazar Todorović.
